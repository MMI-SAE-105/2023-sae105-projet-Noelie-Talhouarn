Hunger Games, the science fiction film

On March 21, 2021, the first Hunger Games movie was released in cinemas. Lasting 2 hours and 22 minutes, this action, adventure and sci-fi movie has conquered the audience.

Training the players

Two months before the shoot, all the young actors of Hunger Games had to participate in a training to transform them, physically and mentally. The program included running, jumping, climbing, weapons, etc. Jennifer Lawrence was trained in archery by an Olympic archer. Josh Hutcherson gained seven kilos of muscle for his role as Peeta.

The main actors

peeta mellark et katniss everdeen

The main actors are Jennifer Lawrence, josh hutcherson, liam hemsworth who bring to life the characters of Katniss Everdeen, Peeta Mellark, and Gale Hawthorne.

Dive into the heart of the Hunger Games universe

To sum up, the Hunger Games are a televised event where the tributes must compete until death, the survivor is declared the winner. The film follows the story of Katniss Everdeen, played by Jennifer Lawrence, a District 12 girl who volunteers for the Games to protect her sister, Prim. She is accompanied by Peeta Mellark, played by Josh Hutcherson, a boy from the same district.

A message of solidarity

This story conveys a message of solidarity. Solidarity is shown as the only way to survive atrocious situations. The rejection of violence is put forward as a way to fight against oppression.

Denouncing reality TV

In addition to its social criticism, Hunger Games denounces two aspects of reality TV. It stages the spectacle of violence with a humiliation of a candidate live on TV and the falsity of the relations between candidates but also the public.

Breaking down stereotypes

This film goes beyond society's gendered stereotypes. Usually in action films, it's the men who are strong. Here, the roles are reversed. We find a strong, active heroine, Katniss Everdeen, taking charge of her family. The heroine is masculine, and her behavior is valued in the film; societal norms are reversed. The film also features a male lead character, Peeta,whose behavior is based on feminine values, romantic, generous and non-violent. These behaviors are valued in the film. However, both also have gender-typical qualities: Katniss cares for others and Peeta is physically strong. Hunger Games goes beyond gender stereotypes, with a strong woman and a softer man.

A Remarkable Cinematic Adaptation

The Hunger Games film is therefore an excellent, faithful and powerful film adaptation of Suzanne Collins' book.
