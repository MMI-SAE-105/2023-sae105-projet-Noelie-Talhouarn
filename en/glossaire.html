Glossary

The capitol

Capitol Hill is the capital of Panem. It's a technologically advanced city where the nation's richest and most powerful people live.

Katniss Everdeen

Katniss Everdeen is the heroine of the Hunger Games trilogy. During the Harvest of the 74th Hunger Games, her little sister, Prim, is drawn. To save her from certain death, Katniss volunteers in her place.

Peeta Mellark

Peeta Mellark is the main male character in the Hunger Games saga. He is played in the films by Josh Hutcherson.
